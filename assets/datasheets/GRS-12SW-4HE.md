# GRS 12SW-4HE — 12" High Excursion Subwoofer

## Overview

GRS 12SW-4HE is a 12-inch high excursion subwoofer with a reinforced paper cone
and rubber surround. Selected as the woofer for the Mk3 Reference Loudspeaker
(v8), replacing the GRS 8SW-4HE-8 from v3-v7. Two units are used per enclosure
in a push-push configuration on the side panels.

With Linkwitz Transform (Fc → 28 Hz, Qtc → 0.707):
- Raw Fc 39 Hz → LT target 28 Hz
- Boost at 20 Hz: ~6 dB
- Max SPL @ 30 Hz: 109.5 dB (half-space, 1m, excursion-limited)

## Displacement

- Single driver: Vd = 6.3 cm³ (Sd 504 × Xmax 12.5 mm)
- Push-push pair: Vd = 12.6 cm³
- 6.3× more displacement than the previous GRS 8SW-4HE-8 pair (2.0 cm³)
- +16 dB max SPL at 30 Hz vs previous design

## Push-push Installation

Two 12SW-4HE are mounted opposed on the left and right side panels (370 mm deep).
With 284 mm cutout on 370 mm panel: 43 mm margin per side. Basket depth ~136 mm
each; internal width between side panels is 276 mm (300 - 2×22), leaving ~4 mm
gap between opposed magnet back plates. A coupling block bonds across the gap
for rigid mechanical coupling (push-push vibration cancellation).

# ScanSpeak H2606/920000 — Datasheet Reference

**Source:** [Official ScanSpeak datasheet PDF](./H2606-920000.pdf) + STEP/IGES/Parasolid CAD files + mounting drawing.

**Driver family:** Horn dome tweeter, 25 mm coated textile dome, 6 Ω.
**Role in mk2:** Tweeter (primary design). Horn exit couples to WG212 waveguide.

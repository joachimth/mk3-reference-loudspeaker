# ScanSpeak 15W/4434G00 — Datasheet Reference

**Source:** [Official ScanSpeak product page](https://www.scan-speak.dk/product/15w-4434g00/) + [official datasheet PDF (319KB)](./15W-4434G00.pdf) + ScanSpeak mounting drawing (embedded in PDF, extracted visually).

**Driver family:** Discovery series, 15 cm midwoofer, 4 Ω.
**Role in mk2:** Midrange driver.

## Mechanical dimensions (from official mounting drawing)

| Parameter | Value | Tolerance | Symbol / use in SCAD |
|---|---|---|---|
| Faceplate outer diameter | **149.3 mm** | ±0.5 | `mid_face_d` |
| Mounting BCD ("Pitch diam", 4-hole pattern) | **136.5 mm** | — | `mid_bcd` |
| Mounting hole diameter | **5.3 mm** | ±0.1 (×4 holes, 90° apart) | `mid_hole_d` |
| Number of mounting holes | **4** | — | `mid_holes` |
| Total depth (incl. front flange) | **61.9 mm** | — | `mid_depth` |
| Front flange thickness | **4.8 mm** | — | `mid_flange_t` (flush-mount rebate depth) |
| Basket / magnet system OD behind flange | **114 mm** (+2/0), Ø90 inner | — | `mid_basket_d` |
| Effective diaphragm diameter | 101 mm | — | (T-S only, not a mounting dim) |
| Cabinet displacement volume | **0.23 L** | — | `mid_displ_L` (datasheet "Mechanical Data") |
| Baffle cutout diameter | **not specified on drawing** — ~Ø123 estimate | — | `mid_cut_d` (must clear the Ø114 basket AND leave screw land inside the Ø136.5 BCD; caliper-verify) |

> **Source-of-truth:** values re-extracted July 2026 directly from the dimension
> drawing in `15W-4434G00.pdf`. An earlier version of this table (104.0 / 95.0 /
> 45.1 / Ø72 "rear cutout") did **not** match the official drawing — the Ø72
> figure led to a wrong `mid_cut_d` in `cabinet.scad` (a Ø72 hole is smaller than
> the Ø114 basket, so the driver could never be mounted). Ø149.3 faceplate,
> Ø136.5 pitch circle and 61.9 mm depth are printed on the drawing.

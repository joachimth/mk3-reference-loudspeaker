# Chapter 1 - Project Goals and Philosophy

This chapter describes the goals, inspiration and overall design philosophy behind Mk2 Reference Loudspeaker.

## Main goals

- Neutral sound
- Excellent spinorama
- Smooth directivity
- Low distortion
- Active DSP
- Long-term maintainability

## Inspiration

- Genelec 8361
- Dutch & Dutch 8C
- Revel Salon2

## Philosophy

The project prioritizes smooth total radiated sound power and controlled directivity over simply optimizing on-axis response.
